Trimmed:    legs = R+Ron/2  |  rungs = 2R+Ron (unchanged)  →  ratio = (2R+Ron)/(R+Ron/2) = exactly 2 ✓
Trim resistors (Ron/2 each, added in series with every leg) need only 1% tolerance — they correct a systematic error, not a precision one.
